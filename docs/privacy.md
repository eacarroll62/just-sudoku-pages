# Privacy Policy for Just Sudoku

**Last updated:** December 2025

Just Sudoku does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
- No personal information is collected
- No analytics
- No advertising identifiers
- No tracking
- No third-party SDKs that collect data

## Game Data
- Game progress, statistics, and preferences are stored **locally on your device**
- This data never leaves your device

## Daily Puzzles
- Completion status and best times are stored locally
- No server communication occurs
